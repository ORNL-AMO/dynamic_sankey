The name of your node, the value and the units, as well as the converted units.

The next section will be defining your Source and Destination Nodes

You can then choose a name, value, units, and converted units.

Hit the "Add Source and Destination" button to create as many nodes as needed

Clicking the bottom bar will allow you to change your node to any color!

Once you have finished creating your nodes you can add links

You can then choose the source #, destination #, the value, units, and the converted units.

Create more links by clicking the "Add Source and Destination Link" button

Once you have completed the last step of adding your links you can then

render your Sankey Diagram by pressing the "Render Sankey" button

Once you have your Sankey rendered you can press the "Download Sankey" button

which allows you to download a picture of your Sankey anywhere on your computer

By doing this, you a picture of your Sankey Diagram should appear based off of the number that you input.
