Team 10 at Tennessee Technological University has partnered with Oak Ridge National to provide a web-based application that will allow engineers and researchers to create Sankey Diagrams. We have created a help tutorial page. Our hope is that this will allow you to better navigate the application!

Step-by-step guide to navigating the Sankey Application

If you wish to access this section–The Help Section–
there is a Help button on the main page that will allow you to navigate to here.

  1. Start by giving your Sankey Diagram a name



  2. Next, you can choose a background to have on your Sankey Diagram



  3. Afterwards, you can choose what type of Sankey you prefer to have: Nodes or Arrows



  4. Finally, you can choose whether you wish to have color gradient or not



  5. You can also make your graph more appealing by adding Measurements and/or Primary Units

Add Sources and Destinations

  1. In the Add Sources and Destination, click on the button and proceed to insert–
    The name of your node, the value and the units, as well as the converted units.
